How we see green energy

Generate. Store. Share

GreenLab is the world’s first truly green business park. We generate sustainable energy, supply it to the businesses located here, and transform it into electrofuels.

In the business park, everything is connected by the SymbiosisNet™ – an intelligent grid of energy and data that lets companies share their surplus energy.
